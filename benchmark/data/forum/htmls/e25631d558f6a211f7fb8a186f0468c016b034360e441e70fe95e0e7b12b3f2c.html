      How to use the Forums- magiciansandmagic.com.

      These forums are for all users of this site.

      1. They are divided into the Member forums. (you can create a forum for your Group when a member of the site- you may want a private group forum – which only members of the group can see).
      2. The Sitewide forums. This everyone can see, even if they are not logged into the site as a member. Let see how the forums go, we can always change the rules as we go along. However, having to be logged in to post a topic or reply to a topic… will hopefully limit getting a lot of spam. Which does no one any good, as it blocks response times, slows down the site and just irritates everyone.

      Enjoy the forums, have your say… if you want to talk to me privately… then using the Kenny@magiciansandmagic.com email address, is probably going to get the fastest response.
